Answer : Hypothesis testing allows the researcher to determine whether the data from the sample is statistically significant

4. What is the significance of the p-value?
Answer : A p-value is a measure of the probability that an observed difference could have occurred just by random chance. The lower the p-value, the greater the statistical significance of the observed difference
